Hi, my name is Gabby and I am a 20 year old student currently studying Animal Health and Welfare at Harper Adams University.

I have several years of experience working with dogs, horses and other animals. This experience includes dog walking, pet sitting, stable work and dog grooming. I also own a cat and a dog myself — I am currently on my placement year working at a canine hydrotherapy and physiotherapy centre.

My dream is to become a veterinary physiotherapist and help animals of all sizes. I am experienced with handling all types of animals and breeds including those who might be extremely energetic, nervous or reactive.

The degree I am currently pursuing, along with my hands-on experience, provides me with the knowledge and skills to meet the needs of every animal.
